Muthoga Kanyari

Founder & Executive Director

Muthoga Kanyari founded SkyVast Green Initiative after a single encounter changed the course of his life: two girls in Diani, neither older than nine, walking over ten kilometres for water from a dried-up riverbed. Unable to look away, he drove them home — and resolved to respond.

Back in the United States he gathered like-minded partners and built an organisation that refuses to accept drought as destiny. Today he leads SGI's vision of engineering self-reliant, women- and youth-led economies across the ASALs — pairing global capital with local delivery and insisting that every outcome be measured, not assumed.

Co-Founder

Director of Field Operations · Kenya

A co-founder of SkyVast Green Initiative, she anchors the organisation's presence on the ground in Kenya. From water pans to women's enterprise groups, she translates strategy into delivery — mobilising communities, building local ownership and making sure the people SGI serves are the people who lead the work.

Her conviction is simple: resilience that isn't community-owned doesn't last. She spends as much time listening in villages as she does planning in the office — and it shows in projects that endure long after the build is done.

Board Chairman

Governance & Financial Stewardship

Our Board Chairman is a seasoned banking professional with over 25 years directing the operational functions of leading banks, and currently consults in the Blue Economy's fishing sector. He brings rigorous financial management, portfolio and project oversight, and a proven record of turning bold ideas into disciplined execution.

At SGI he safeguards governance and financial integrity — ensuring that every dollar entrusted to us is stewarded with the same rigour he brought to the boardroom, and that our strategy stays both ambitious and accountable.

Project Co-ordinator

Field Lead · Kwale County

He leads SGI's project team in Kwale County, where our story began. From dawn site visits to community trainings, he turns plans into built infrastructure — coordinating local labour, contractors and water-user groups so that tanks, pans and reforestation move from blueprint to reality.

Equal parts engineer, organiser and neighbour, he's the reason delivery in the field stays on time, on budget and community-owned.

Director of Programs

Program Design & Delivery

Our Director of Programs converts SGI's six-pillar model into deployable projects across nine counties. From scoping a village's water reality to sequencing build, training and enterprise launch, she ensures every deployment runs the full Illuminate-to-Multiply cycle.
