Chapter 01 — The encounter

Two girls. Nine years old.
Ten kilometres for water
from a riverbed that had
run dry.

Diani, Kwale County, 2017. We drove them home — and we could not look away. From that moment, SkyVast Green Initiative was born: a promise that no child's future should be measured by the distance to water.
