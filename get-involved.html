Good to know

Donor questions, answered

Yes. SkyVast Green Initiative is a U.S.-registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, so donations from U.S. taxpayers are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. Our dual presence means your gift is both deductible and delivered directly on the ground in Kenya.
Every project is technology-verified — real-time dashboards, GPS-tagged trees and AI tracking. You receive documentation showing exactly what your contribution built, where, and for how many people. We report verified outcomes, not estimates.
They're scales of the same model: Starter (~$500, one household tank), Community (~$50K, a village's shared systems and reforestation), Golden (~$250K, multi-village cluster), and Resilience ($1M+, county-scale dam and ecosystem infrastructure). Each advances all six pillars.
Absolutely. Golden and Resilience Packs support named partnerships and co-branding, paired with ESG-grade reporting. Our "Beans to Trees" program is a ready template for corporate sustainability goals. Talk to our partnerships team.
Yes — use the Monthly toggle in the donation panel above. Recurring gifts let us plan deployments with confidence and stretch every dollar further across the five-phase model.
